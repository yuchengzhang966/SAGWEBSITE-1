Program Overview

The Consulting Careers Program is designed to prepare you for a successful career in management consulting. Whether you're a recent graduate, career switcher, or experienced professional looking to break into consulting, this program will equip you with the skills, knowledge, and confidence needed to excel in the highly competitive consulting recruitment process.

Led by former consultants from top firms like McKinsey, BCG, and Bain (MBB), our program focuses on developing the core competencies that consulting firms look for in candidates:

Structured Problem Solving

Learn how to break down complex business problems into manageable components using proven frameworks and methodologies.

Quantitative Analysis

Develop the ability to quickly analyze numerical data, identify trends, and draw meaningful insights.

Communication Skills

Master the art of presenting your analysis and recommendations clearly and persuasively to clients.

Business Acumen

Build a solid understanding of business concepts, industry dynamics, and strategic thinking.

Program Curriculum

Our comprehensive curriculum covers all aspects of consulting case interviews and career preparation.

Module 1: Case Interview Fundamentals

  • Introduction to case interview formats (candidate-led vs. interviewer-led)
  • The PARADE framework for structuring cases
  • Common business frameworks (Profitability, Market Entry, Pricing, etc.)
  • Skills practice: Issue trees and hypothesis-driven problem solving
  • Assignment: Analyze a business case using multiple frameworks

Module 2: Quantitative Analysis & Business Math

  • Mental math techniques for case interviews
  • Market sizing and estimation approaches
  • Profitability analysis and driver trees
  • Data interpretation and insight generation
  • Assignment: Complete timed market sizing and profitability exercises

Module 3: Advanced Case Techniques

  • Firm-specific case approaches (McKinsey, BCG, Bain, Big 4)
  • Charts and exhibits analysis
  • Creative problem solving for non-standard cases
  • Industry-specific cases (Healthcare, Tech, Retail, Financial Services)
  • Assignment: Solve advanced cases with limited information

Module 4: Communication & Leadership

  • Structured communication techniques
  • Presentation skills for final recommendations
  • Managing client interactions and stakeholder management
  • Behavioral interview preparation (leadership, teamwork, impact)
  • Assignment: Record and analyze your communication in a mock presentation

Module 5: Personal Branding & Application Strategy

  • Resume optimization for consulting
  • Cover letter strategies that get attention
  • Networking techniques for consulting recruitment
  • Interview preparation beyond cases (fit interviews, PEI, etc.)
  • Assignment: Develop your consulting application materials

FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

Everything You Need to Know

Answers to common questions about our Consulting Careers Program.

What types of consulting firms do your students get into?

Our students have received offers from a wide range of consulting firms, including MBB (McKinsey, Bain, BCG), Big 4 (Deloitte, PwC, EY, KPMG), tier-2 strategy firms (Strategy&, Oliver Wyman, L.E.K.), and boutique consultancies. We tailor our training to match the specific interview styles of your target firms.

How does your program differ from other consulting prep services?

Unlike many programs that offer generic advice or one-size-fits-all training, our coaching is completely personalized to your background, learning style, and target firms. Our coaches are all former consultants who have both worked at and recruited for top firms, giving them insider knowledge of what makes candidates successful. We focus not just on frameworks but on developing the underlying skills and thought processes that make exceptional consultants.

What is your success rate?

Over 80% of our students who complete the full program receive at least one offer from their target consulting firms within 3 months. This is significantly higher than the industry average acceptance rate of approximately 1-3%. However, success depends on many factors including your background, target firms, and commitment to the program.

Can I complete this program while working full-time?

Yes, the program is designed to be flexible and accommodate working professionals. Most coaching sessions can be scheduled in the evenings or weekends. You'll need to dedicate approximately 5-10 hours per week for self-study and practice in addition to the scheduled coaching sessions.

Do you offer any guarantees?

While we can't guarantee job offers (no ethical program can), we do guarantee the quality of our coaching. If you feel that a particular coaching session didn't meet your expectations, we'll provide a complimentary replacement session with another coach. Our reputation depends on your success, and we're deeply invested in helping you achieve your goals.

How much does the program cost?

Program costs vary based on the specific package and level of support you need. Our basic consulting program starts at $3,000, with more comprehensive packages available. During your free consultation, we'll discuss options that fit your needs and budget. We also offer payment plans to make the investment more manageable.
